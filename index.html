Instructions

If the number on the left side is greater than the number on the right side, please press keyboard_arrow_left key. If the number on the right side is greater than the number on the left side, please press the keyboard_arrow_right key. After each keypress, watch the data chart on the right update!

Chart Information

The above chart records and displays the number of correct responses versus incorrect responses. The data is updated in real time; chart functionality is provided by Chart.JS.
